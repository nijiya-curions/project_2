Frequently Asked Questions

How do industrial robots work?
Industrial robots use advanced algorithms and sensors to automate tasks traditionally performed by humans.
What are the main applications of industrial robots?
Common applications include welding, assembly, packaging, and material handling.
How can industrial robots benefit businesses?
Industrial robots increase efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve product consistency.
What types of industries commonly use industrial robots?
Industries such as automotive, electronics, food processing, and pharmaceuticals use industrial robots extensively.
